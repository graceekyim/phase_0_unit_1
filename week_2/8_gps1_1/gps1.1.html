PAIR PROGRAMMING 101

Why is pair-programming important to DBC?

  • Opens up the possibility of different solutions
  • Real world experience -- many companies use pair-programming in their offices
  • Facilitates collaboration


What does pair-programming mean?

  • Exchanging ideas
  • Collaborating on projects


What's the difference between a driver and a navigator?

  • Driver - types, discusses and executes directions from navigator
  • Navigator - directs design and content flow


Reflections on Pairing

  • Less stressful than expected
  • Helpful to learn new tricks
  • Learned someone else's aesthetics
  • Difficult to know who should make the decisions about style/content
